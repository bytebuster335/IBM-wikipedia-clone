Introduction

International Business Machines Corporation (IBM) is an American multinational technology company headquartered in Armonk, New York, with operations in over 170 countries.

History

IBM was founded in 1911 as the Computing-Tabulating-Recording Company (CTR) and was renamed to IBM in 1924. IBM has been a major contributor to computing innovations such as the hard disk drive, the floppy disk, and the ATM.

Products and Services

IBM offers products in cloud computing, artificial intelligence, computer hardware, and software. It is also known for its Watson AI platform and quantum computing research.
